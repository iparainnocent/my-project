piano

Whereras the professional field of music education pertains to the teaching of music in classrooms or group settings, piano pedagody focuses on the teaching of musical skills to individual piano students. This is often done via private or semiprivate instructions, commonly referred to as piano lessons.

Drum set

We start by teaching students the different parts of their drum kit, as well as correct posture and grip. Studdents then start to learn basic drumming patterns, also known as rudiments. At the same time, we teach students how to keep a beat, control tempo, and read music.

Guiter

On guitar, we know some common goals for beginners include learning basic cords and strumming patterns, developing finger dexterity and hand cordination, and playing simple songs. As you progress, you may want to focus on more advanced techniques such as improvisation, music theory, and performance skills.

Musical notes

Regarding musical notes lessons, knowing how to read notes on sheet music might seem tricky initially, but its easier than it looks. From bottom to top, the treble clef notes for the lines are E, G, B, D, F; and the space notes are F, A, C, E. The spaces, in the case are easy to memorize since they spell out the word face.
